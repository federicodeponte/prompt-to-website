**Impact:** Websites look incomplete, not production-ready

7. **Features Grid 1** - Core features (3-4)
8. **Video/Demo** - Product walkthrough
9. **Features Grid 2** - Advanced features (3-4)
10. **Use Cases** - Different scenarios
11. **Testimonials Carousel** - Customer stories
12. **Pricing** - Plans and tiers
13. **Comparison Table** - vs competitors
14. **Integration Showcase** - Compatibility
15. **Security/Trust** - Badges, certifications
16. **FAQ** - Common questions
17. **CTA Section 1** - Start free trial
18. **Blog/Resources** - Latest content
19. **Team** - About us
20. **Newsletter** - Stay updated

22. **Footer** - Links, social, legal
